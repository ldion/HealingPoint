About Kristin

Kristin Ward graduated with a Masters of Science in Traditional Oriental Medicine (MSTOM) from Pacific College of Oriental Medicine (PCOM) in San Diego. Following PCOM, Mrs. Ward studied at Chengdu University Hospital, one of China's largest teaching hospitals located in the Sichuan Province of China. She is presently earning her Doctorate in Oriental Medicine specializing in Mental Health, Geriatrics, Orthopedics, Neurology, Women's Health, Preventive Medicine, and Pediatrics.

Kristin currently lives in Middletown, RI. She is certified as a Diplomate of Oriental Medicine by the NCCAOM, a Doctor of Acupuncture in Rhode Island, and a Licensed Acupuncturist in Massachusetts and California.

After suffering from chronic Lyme Disease for over 10 years during her childhood and early adulthood, Kristin has personally witnessed the difficulties of chronic illness and the healing effects of Oriental Medicine. She is passionate about sharing her knowledge with her patients to empower them in achieving their own ideal state of health.
