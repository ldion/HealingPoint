Our Services

Traditional Oriental Medicine has been practiced for over 4,000 years. Oriental medicine emphasizes a holistic approach and treats the core cause of an illness. This approach allows for effective treatments as well as for preventative care.

We offer all modalities of Traditional Oriental Medicine:

  • Acupuncture
  • Moxibustion
  • Cupping
  • Gua Sha
  • Chinese Herbology
  • Tui Na
  • Dietary and Lifestyle Recommendations

What We Treat

According to "Acupuncture: The NIH Consensus Statement", put out by the U.S. National Institute of Health (NIH) in 1997, acupuncture is very useful for treating many conditions with minimal adverse side effects. In addition, the NIH recommends U.S. insurance companies to provide full coverage of acupuncture treatment for certain conditions.

The World Health Organization (WHO) of the United Nations identified over 40 medical conditions effectively treated by acupuncture. This list includes:

  • All Pain and Musculoskeletal Disorders
  • Neurological Disorders
  • Sciatica and Back Pain
  • Paralysis, Bell's Palsy
  • Gynecology- PMS, Menopause, Infertility
  • Pediatric Diseases
  • Digestive Issues
  • Morning Sickness
  • Obesity
  • Dermatological Issues
  • Headaches, Migraines
  • Psycho-emotional Disorders
  • Stress, Anxiety, Depression
  • Disorders of Ears, Nose and Throat
  • Asthma and Allergies
  • Addiction- Smoking, Drugs, Alcohol
  • Chemotherapy side-effects
  • Chronic Fatigue and Fibromyalgia
